innovations

Chattem has always been an innovative company, and we continue to push the boundaries of what’s possible in production, marketing and consumer interaction. We have recently won awards for our product packaging and had products named Product of the Year as recently as 2015. In addition, we have created innovative, interactive screens on retail shelves that help consumers better understand our brands and offerings.
